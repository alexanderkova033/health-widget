# General micro-tips shown across morning/afternoon/evening, one per line.
Try the 20-20-20 rule: every 20 minutes, look 20 feet away for 20 seconds.
Blink consciously — we blink far less while staring at screens.
Stand up, stretch your legs, and drop your shoulders.
Un-cross your legs and roll your ankles right now.
Take 3 slow, deep breaths. Slow breathing can help calm your nervous system.
Match screen brightness to your room to reduce eye strain.
A quick 5-minute walk is a great way to refresh your focus.
Aim for at least 3 screen-free breaks during your study blocks.
Keep a glass of water on your desk so sipping is effortless.
